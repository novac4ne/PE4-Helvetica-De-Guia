FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Before You Contact Us

Is Helvetica free?

No. Helvetica is a commercial typeface owned by Monotype. It requires a license for professional use.

Why is Helvetica so popular?

Because of its neutrality, excellent readability, and versatility across print and digital media.

What inspired this website?

This website demonstrates minimalist web design while celebrating one of the most influential typefaces ever created.
