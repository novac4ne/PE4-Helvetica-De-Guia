INTRODUCTION

Simplicity That Outlived Trends.

Neutral

Helvetica was created to remain unbiased. Instead of expressing emotion, it lets the content speak for itself.

Timeless

From subway signs to corporate identities, Helvetica has remained relevant for decades, proving that good design rarely becomes obsolete.

Universal

Used by designers, governments, businesses, and artists around the world, Helvetica became the visual language of modern communication.

DESIGN PRINCIPLE

Less Decoration. More Communication.

Helvetica became the foundation of Swiss graphic design because it values readability, consistency, and structure above ornament. Its clean appearance allows information to remain the primary focus.
